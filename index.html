Slieve Corragh

( Silabh Corrach )

Slieve Corragh (SLEEV KORRA [needs IPA] ; from Irish Sliabh Corrach, meaning '"rugged/pointed mountain" [2] or "sheep hill" [3]') is one of the Mourne Mountains in County Down, Northern Ireland. [1] It has a height of 640 metres (2,100 ft). [1]
